Welcome to Luxembourg

The Network Science and Economics Conference brings together economists, computer scientists, and applied mathematicians working on networks — how they form, what flows across them, and how they shape markets, institutions, and behavior.

In 2026 the series crosses the Atlantic for the first time. We are delighted to host the conference at the University of Luxembourg, whose Belval campus sits at the heart of Europe — a short ride from Luxembourg City and a natural crossroads for the field's growing European and international community.

The conference is part of a series that now meets twice each year. We look forward to welcoming you for three days of talks, posters, and conversation.

At a glance

Three days of plenaries, contributed talks, and posters on the economics of networks.
